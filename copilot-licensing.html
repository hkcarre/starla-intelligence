Key Distinctions (Senior Expert Summary)

Productivity vs Automation Platform: Microsoft 365 Copilot enhances knowledge work directly inside Office apps using enterprise data. Copilot Chat is the conversational entry point that scales from basic AI chat to enterprise-grounded intelligence when backed by a Copilot licence. Copilot Studio is a platform for developers and citizen makers to build workflow-centric AI agents that can integrate across systems — not just inside Office.

Licensing Models: Per-user (Microsoft 365 Copilot assigned user by user) vs Capacity/Consumption-based (Copilot Studio charged via Copilot Credits: prepaid packs or pay-as-you-go).

Scope of Deployment: Internal Workflows (M365 Copilot boosts individual productivity and team collaboration) vs Enterprise Automation (Copilot Studio scales AI beyond individual productivity — e.g., custom service bots, cross-system orchestrators, handoff workflows).
